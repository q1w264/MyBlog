隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

1. Cookie 自动收集说明

当你访问本网站时，系统可能会自动收集部分技术信息，例如访问时间、浏览器类型、设备信息、访问页面与来源链接。这些信息通常通过 Cookie、日志文件或类似技术生成，用于保障网站正常运行、统计访问趋势与优化内容体验。

When you visit this website, certain technical information may be collected automatically, such as visit time, browser type, device information, visited pages, and referral links. This information is typically generated through cookies, log files, or similar technologies and is used to maintain website operation, analyze traffic trends, and improve content experience.

2. 用户主动提交的数据处理

当你通过评论、邮件或其他方式主动提交信息（如昵称、邮箱地址、留言内容）时，本网站仅在实现沟通反馈、内容互动或必要的管理场景下处理相关数据。除非法律法规另有要求，或经你明确授权，本网站不会将该等信息用于与上述目的无关的用途。

When you voluntarily submit information via comments, email, or other channels (such as nickname, email address, or message content), this website will process such data only for communication, interaction, or necessary administration purposes. Unless otherwise required by law or explicitly authorized by you, such information will not be used for unrelated purposes.

3. 限制利用原则

本网站遵循最小必要与目的限定原则，仅在实现声明目的所必需的范围内使用相关信息，不进行超范围、无关或过度处理。对于可能涉及的第三方服务（如统计、评论系统），将尽量选择合规方案，并要求其在合理范围内履行数据保护义务。

This website follows the principles of data minimization and purpose limitation. Information is used only to the extent necessary for the stated purposes, and no excessive or unrelated processing is intended. For third-party services that may be involved (such as analytics or comment systems), compliant solutions are preferred, and they are expected to fulfill data protection obligations within a reasonable scope.

4. 信息安全保障

本网站采取合理的技术与管理措施，以降低数据丢失、泄露、篡改或被未授权访问的风险。但请理解，互联网传输与存储不存在绝对安全。本网站将持续改进安全实践，并在发现潜在风险时及时采取处置措施。

This website adopts reasonable technical and organizational measures to reduce risks of data loss, leakage, tampering, or unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission or storage can be guaranteed as absolutely secure. Security practices will be continuously improved, and appropriate actions will be taken once potential risks are identified.

5. 适用范围与更新

本隐私政策适用于 https://www.q1w264.com 所展示的个人技术博客内容。若业务形态、功能模块或法律要求发生变化，本政策可能更新；更新版本发布后即生效。

This Privacy Policy applies to the personal technology blog content published at https://www.q1w264.com. This policy may be updated if business forms, functional modules, or legal requirements change; updated versions take effect upon publication.

6. 管辖法律

The establishment, performance, and interpretation of this policy shall be governed by the laws of Mainland China.
